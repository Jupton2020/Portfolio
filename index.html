About Me

Hello! I am Jaqueline Upton, I was born and raised in Sao Paulo,Brazil. I moved to the US in 2016 to learn english and to have a new cultural experience. I fell in love with the beautiful PNW and I have been living here with my husband ever since. My first language is Portuguese but I also speak English and Spanish. The best place to find me on a sunny day is on Alki beach or in the mountains.
